Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes Mod Lyrics

On the Subject of Lyrics

It's kinda difficult to sing along when you don't even know what your hearing.

This module has a display, a button that says "Start Lyrics", and a small white button.

The module is initially in play mode, where upon pressing the "Start Lyrics" button a set of lyrics from a song in the table below will start playing. The lyrics will appear one at a time, and some will be missing.

Pressing the small white button will put the module in submit mode, where the display will show bars representing the missing characters in the first of the missing lyrics. Pressing the small white button again will put the module back into play mode.

To solve this module, input and submit the missing lyrics in the order they appear using your keyboard. The display will update upon each keyboard press to show the current input. Pressing backspace will remove the last character inputted and pressing enter will submit the input. The next missing lyric will only appear after a correct submission.

Submitting an incorrect missing lyric will cause a strike and the module will NOT reset.
